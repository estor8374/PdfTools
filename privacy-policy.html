Privacy Policy

Last updated: November 17, 2025

Your privacy is the most important part of our service. This policy outlines how we handle your data (or rather, how we *don't*).

1. File Security & In-Browser Processing

This is the most important section of our policy:

All tools on DevWhim.xyz operate entirely within your web browser. When you use our tools (like splitting, merging, or converting a PDF), your files are **NEVER** uploaded to our servers, or any third-party server.

The entire process of reading, manipulating, and saving your file happens locally on your own computer. We do not have access to, nor can we see, view, or store your files. This makes our service as secure as any offline desktop software.

2. Information We Collect

We are committed to collecting as little information as possible.

  • Personal Information: We do not collect any personally identifiable information. We do not require registration, usernames, or email addresses to use our tools.
  • Usage Data (Analytics): We use Google Analytics to collect anonymous, aggregated data about how visitors use our site. This includes information like which tools are most popular, how long users stay on a page, and what type of browser they are using. This data helps us improve the website and is not linked to any personal information.

3. Cookies

We use cookies for a few reasons:

  • Google Analytics: To help us understand website traffic (as mentioned above).
  • Google AdSense: To serve personalized ads. Google and its partners use cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to this and other websites.

You can opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google's Ads Settings.

4. Advertising Partners

We use Google AdSense to display ads, which helps keep our tools 100% free. These third-party ad vendors may use cookies and web beacons to collect non-personally identifiable information about your visits to this and other sites in order to provide advertisements about goods and services of interest to you.

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will post any changes on this page, so please review it periodically.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us.
